About GastricCare

Fighting Gastric Cancer Through Early Detection

We are a dedicated team of healthcare professionals, data scientists, and technologists committed to making gastric cancer screening accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

Making Early Detection Accessible to All

Gastric cancer is one of the leading causes of cancer-related deaths worldwide, yet early detection can significantly improve survival rates. Our mission is to leverage the power of artificial intelligence to provide accessible, accurate, and immediate risk assessments.

We believe that everyone deserves access to quality healthcare screening, regardless of their location or economic status. Through our platform, we aim to democratize gastric cancer screening and save lives through early detection.
